How It Works

Each volume drops at 7:00 AM ET. Format rotates by day:

  • Monday — The Week Ahead. What landed over the weekend; what to brace for.
  • Wednesday — Deep Dive. One long Must Read, one Radar piece.
  • Friday — The Wrap. Multi-bullet roundup of the week's biggest fights.

Every issue carries On the Frontlines — state-tagged dispatches from the planning-commission, town-board, and PUC level. Friday issues add Plays of the Week, surfacing notable community-engagement tactics from peer firms, opposition coalitions, developers, and regulators.
